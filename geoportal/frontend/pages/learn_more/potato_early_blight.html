Potato playbook

Early blight suppression blueprint

Alternaria solani thrives under warm temperatures (24–29°C) and leaf wetness periods longer than 12 hours. Crop stress, hail, or mechanical damage accelerate lesion growth.

Scouting signals

  • Target lesions (brown concentric rings) starting on lower canopy.
  • Premature defoliation on K-deficient soils.
  • Needle-like sporulation under UV flashlight.

GeoPortal recommendations

  1. Deploy protectant fungicides before forecasted humidity spikes.
  2. Maintain N:K ratios at 1:1.2 during bulking stage.
  3. Upload canopy imagery weekly—Crop Lab tracks lesion velocity.
