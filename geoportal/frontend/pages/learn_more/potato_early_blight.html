Potato playbook

Early blight suppression blueprint

Alternaria solani thrives in warm (24–29 °C) canopies with leaf wetness greater than 12 hours. Stress, hail, and mechanical injury accelerate lesion expansion.

Scouting signals

  • Target lesions with brown concentric rings emerge on lower canopy.
  • K-deficient parcels defoliate prematurely.
  • Needle-like sporulation visible under UV flashlight.

GeoPortal recommendations

  1. Deploy protectant fungicides before humidity spikes forecasted by the dashboard.
  2. Maintain N:K ratios at 1:1.2 during tuber bulking.
  3. Upload canopy imagery weekly — Crop Lab tracks lesion velocity.
