Wheat shield

Stripe rust containment protocol

Puccinia striiformis overwinters on volunteer wheat and barley. Cool nights (5–12°C) plus moist mornings trigger rapid pustule expansion.

Risk assessment

GeoPortal ingests NOAA dewpoint forecasts and in-field leaf wetness sensor data to map spore germination windows. Alerts fire when degree-hours exceed 350.

Control stack

  • Deploy triazole + strobilurin mixes at flag leaf emergence.
  • Maintain 20 m buffer strips with rust-tolerant cultivars.
  • Rotate with chickpea or flax to break the green bridge.
