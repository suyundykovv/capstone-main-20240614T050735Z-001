Wheat shield

Stripe rust containment protocol

Puccinia striiformis survives on volunteer cereals. Cool nights (5–12 °C) and moist mornings accelerate pustule expansion across tillers.

Risk assessment

GeoPortal blends NOAA dew point forecasts with in-field leaf wetness probes to map germination windows. Alerts trigger when degree-hours exceed 350 and when NDVI variance surpasses 0.12.

Control stack

  • Deploy triazole + strobilurin mixes at flag leaf emergence.
  • Maintain 20 m buffer strips with rust-tolerant cultivars.
  • Rotate with chickpea or flax to break the green bridge.
